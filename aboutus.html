READING CLUB


This book club or reading club was formed by a few enthusiastic book lovers in 2020. Quarantine had us all bored and confined to our homes. What is better activity than reading books by ourselves in the comfort of our homes?
Sharing our thoughts and reviews with other people who love reading as much as we do !!

If you are looking for a new book to read, don't worry, we post new lists of good books to our page every month. We also have story telling sessions for different age groups. Kids, teenagers, adults - everyone is welcome here!

Our club is the perfect place to share your views and opinions about the various books you read. You can submit your reviews of our recommended books to us every month. It is of immense help to a new reader.
