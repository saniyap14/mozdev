This is a special club for book lovers. Reading books is a valuable habit. Everyone should cultivate the habit of reading books. Everyone who loves to read or listen to stories is welcome here.


"Storytelling is the most powerful way to put ideas into the world today." --Robert McKee

ACTIVITIES

October book of the month

  1. Book Reviews -
    Read a new book and send your review to us. We upload monthly book lists to this page.

  2. Poetry Reading-
    Have a collection of poems and are looking for a platform to share? Look no further. Join the club and avail our weekly poetry reading sessions.

  3. Storytelling-
    We have weekly story telling sessions as well. Share your stories with us or simply join for a sweet story telling session.

  4. Story Writing Workshops-
    Want to write stories but don't know where to begin? Sign up for a short story writing session that happens in our club.
